Spotlight on… Applied AI
Applied AI — formerly Quantitative Analytics — reflects a deliberate evolution in how Group Control harnesses data science and artificial intelligence to manage risk.
The team designs and deploys machine learning models and agentic AI capabilities that underpin fraud detection, financial crime prevention, and transaction monitoring across the organisation. Rigorous analytical standards and a commitment to innovation ensure that Group Control remains at the forefront of applied data science.
